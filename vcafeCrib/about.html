About Us

Beyond gaming and coffee


V-Café crib is a place where you can come and have a fun time eating and drinking our delicious food/beverages and immerse yourself in our gaming facility's. V-Café crib offers coffee and other related option with a twist, which is that we give our customers options to have a fun time by playing video games with their delicious food.



Introducing great coffee with the latest and greatest gaming equipment


Video games have become popular over the year and their popularity can best be described by fact that in 2016 the video game industry's revenue was more than that of the music and movie industry combined with the total revenue being $23.5 billion dollars. The rise of the video-gaming market has been exponential over they years but still the idea of gaming Is far to expensive for some people and is also not convenient because video games lose their value after a certain amount of time. Taking these issues to account V-café crib a video game and coffee shop hybrid will offer an enjoyable experience with the latest and greatest games at the best value possible. The café part of our store will allow us to sell customers our coffee, energy drinks and snacks which will help our customers feel more comfortable and thus they will have an enjoyable experience. The store will focus on its videogame side more but if people want to just by coffee and snacks like a regular café, they will be able to do that as well. V-café crib will be packed with the latest video game technologies such as high-power gaming computers, PS4, Xbox one and the Nintendo switch. V-café crib will charge it customers by various ways such as having a yearly subscription, hourly subscription or special discounts for professional gaming teams. The hourly charge will allow you to play the games at the store (different amount may apply depending on the gaming platform), but the catch will be that they will have to buy something at the café: this practice will be in charge to ensure that the gaming part of the store does not take over the café part to much. The different drawback of buying an hourly subscription will not be there if you buy a yearly subscription which will ensure you access to all the games and you will not have to buy and order food or coffee. In conclusion V-café crib will offer an enjoyable experience, good food and top of the line gaming equipment at the best price possible.
